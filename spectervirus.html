Specter Virus Overview

SpecterVirus is a true first-person horror shooter. Play as CIA operative Winslow and venture into a quarantined zone to find answers to many questions. Find all research documents in the level to uncover the truth behind all the reports coming in. I am the Combat, UI, Interactable Object, Gameplay, Menus, SFX, VFX, and Player programmer on this.

Specter Virus Details

Target Engine Unreal Engine 5.2.1 Implementation Type Code Based Language C++ Development Duration 3 months Team Size 2 Genre Horror Shooter (True FPS)

Project Scope and Team Dynamics

During the first month, I developed the entire prototype independently, as my assigned teammate was inactive. A producer joined the project in the second month to help with project scope but my teammate again was inactive, followed by two additional developers in the third month who assisted with final implementation. The project concluded at the end of month three.

The light remained off until all research documents were collected

Once completed, it illuminated green to indicate mission success and unlocked the next level
